> Hello! I’m **Marta**, an experienced ESL instructor dedicated to helping adult learners achieve confidence and fluency in English.

## Credentials & Experience

- **B.A. in English** (4.0 GPA)  
- **TESL Certificate**, Oxford Seminars  
- **2,400+ ESL students** taught (as of May 2026)  

## My Language Journey

I was born in Ukraine and moved to the United States at age 10. Because of my experiences in the U.S., I decided to become an English teacher. I encountered many challenges, but that experience will help me relate to you and guide you in your English language experience.

> Now, I’m studying my 5th language, Japanese, and after I'm conversational, I'll start learning my 6th language. 

## Who I Help

This program is ideal for adult learners, especially **Ukrainian**, **Russian**, and **Chinese** speakers who want to improve their Business English through a structured, textbook-based approach, including:

- Professional communication skills
- Business vocabulary and terminology
- Speaking and listening in workplace contexts
- Email writing and formal correspondence
- Presentation and meeting skills

---

## Teaching Style

- **Customized Lessons** tailored to your goals (e.g., living in the U.S., travel, test prep)  
- **Real‑Time Feedback**:
  - Live corrections as you speak  
  - Use of phonetic symbols (for clear pronunciation guidance)  /ˈsɪmb(ə)ls/
